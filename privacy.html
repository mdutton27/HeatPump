Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026

Overview

This privacy policy explains how the Heat Pump Payback Calculator ("we", "us", "the calculator") collects, uses, and protects your personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

We believe in keeping things simple: we only collect what we need, we tell you exactly how we use it, and you can ask us to delete it at any time.

What information we collect

We may collect the following information depending on how you use the calculator:

Calculator inputs

  • Your suburb or location (to estimate climate zone and electricity pricing)
  • Current heating setup and energy bill amounts
  • Home size and insulation details
  • Heat pump preferences and selections

Bill upload data

  • If you upload an electricity bill, we use AI (Anthropic Claude) to extract relevant data such as your usage, rates, and plan details
  • The bill image or file is processed in your current session only and is not stored on our servers after extraction is complete

Contact details (only if you choose to save results or request quotes)

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)

Why we collect this information

We use your information for these purposes:

  • To run the calculator - your inputs are needed to generate your personalised payback estimate
  • To save your results - if you choose to save, we store your calculator results and contact details so you can retrieve them later
  • To send confirmation emails - when you save results, we send you a confirmation email with a link to your saved estimate
  • To connect you with installers - if you request quotes, we share your contact details and calculator results with heat pump installers in your area

AI processing of bill data

When you upload an electricity bill, it is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI service for data extraction. This means:

  • Your bill is processed by Anthropic Claude to read and extract usage figures, rates, and plan details
  • The bill data is used only for extraction during your current session
  • We do not store the uploaded bill image or file beyond the session
  • Extracted data (such as kWh usage and rates) is only saved if you choose to save your results

Anthropic's use of data sent to their API is governed by their own privacy policy and data usage terms. Bill data sent for extraction is not used by Anthropic to train their AI models.

Who we share your information with

Heat pump installers

If you request quotes, we share your name, contact details, suburb, and calculator results with installers in your area so they can provide you with a quote. This only happens when you actively request it.

Energy retailers

We will only share your information with energy retailers if you explicitly opt in via a separate checkbox. This is entirely optional and never pre-selected.

Service providers

We use trusted service providers to operate the calculator (such as hosting and email delivery). These providers only process your data on our behalf and under our instructions.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with any third parties for marketing purposes unless you have explicitly opted in.

How long we keep your information

  • Calculator session data - if you do not save your results, your data is not stored beyond your browser session
  • Saved results and contact details - kept until you request deletion
  • Lead data shared with installers - once shared with an installer, that installer holds a copy under their own privacy policy. We cannot delete data already shared with third parties, but we can delete our copy
  • Uploaded bills - not stored beyond the processing session

Cookies and tracking

We use minimal cookies required for the calculator to function. Specifically:

  • We do not use tracking cookies
  • We do not use analytics or advertising cookies
  • We may use essential cookies to maintain your session while using the calculator

If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and provide you with clear choices.

Your rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access your data - ask us what personal information we hold about you
  • Correct your data - ask us to fix any information that is wrong or out of date
  • Delete your data - ask us to delete the personal information we hold about you

We will respond to your request within 20 working days, as required by the Privacy Act 2020.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, want to make a request about your data, or have a privacy concern, please contact us:

Email: privacy@heatpumpcalculator.co.nz

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to make a complaint to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will note the date of the last update at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
